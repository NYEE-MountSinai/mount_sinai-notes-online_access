Safety Checklist

  • Double Gloves
  • Absorbent pads covering clothing
  • Spill kit nearby
  • Move the ultrasonic cleaner to the wet lab area and ensure its filled with enzymatic cleaner
  • Filled spray bottle with enzymatic cleaner
Note

Nothing leaves or enters the wet lab area unless properly disinfected.

  • Check assembly

    Pre-Assembly

    • Inspect all parts for cracks.
    • Ensure all O-rings are complete with no chips or divots
    • Ensure all parts are available per BOM.

    • Ensure tongue lines up perfectly with the opening in the tip
      of the frenulum to tip of tongue (after sharpened)

    O-Rings

    • Assemble O-rings per the below diagrams
    • INCLUDE 1 ADDITIONAL 1x5 ORING RIGHT AFTER TOP SPRING
  • Ensure there are no air bubbles when siphoning water to the ground
    • Press the button and move the slider while siphoning to check button and valve seal
    • With the gator fully primed and the valve fully forward to stop flow, leave the gator untouched for 30 seconds and ensure the water level in the gators tube to ground does not change in height
  • Dripper is cut open and the tubing is inserted directly onto the drippers exit
    • The filter tab is open
  • All IV tubing is cut from the drippers tubing

  • Gator is primed with its slider fully forward

Procedure Checklist

  • Make side port incision
  • Enable flow in the irrigator using the rolling slider
  • Insert offhand into the eye
  • Make the main incision
  • Perform the capsulorhexis
  • Use the miloop to cutup the lens into 4 or more fragments
  • Insert the gator
  • Move the stop cock to the right to enable high inflow
  • Pull back on the slider all the way to enable outflow
  • remove the cataract
  • remove the gator

New Eye Checklist

  • Remove the tail, the spring, and the mesh

  • Clear out the mesh, tip, and interior of all debris

  • Check and reinsert any missing O-Rings

    • Insert the mesh
    • insert the spring
    • insert and screw on the tail

  • Ensure there are no air bubbles when siphoning water to the ground

    • Press the button and move the slider while siphoning to check button and valve seal

  • The stopcock is off in the direction of the offhand

  • The outlet to the gator is placed in the empty IPA Bucket

  • IPA bucket is empty and all 4 IV bags are completely full

Cleanup Checklist

  • All instruments go in the ultrasonic cleaner with the enzymatic solution on the highest heating setting with the lid on
  • All sharps get disposed of in a sharps container
  • All downstream tubing gets disposed of in a biohazard bin
  • Enzymatic solution is added to the collection container, then all the fluid is emptied and the container is rinsed with enzymatic solution.
  • Eyes are placed in a glove and placed in the tissue fridge
  • All other waste goes in the biohazard bin
  • All surfaces get sprayed and wiped with enzymatic solution
