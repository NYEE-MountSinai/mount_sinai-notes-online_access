Outlier removal

Outliers are being removed using the inter quartile range method.

def remove_outliers(df, column):
    """Remove outliers from a DataFrame column using the IQR method."""
    Q1 = df[column].quantile(0.25)
    Q3 = df[column].quantile(0.75)
    IQR = Q3 - Q1
    lower_bound = Q1 - 1.5 * IQR
    upper_bound = Q3 + 1.5 * IQR
    return df[(df[column] >= lower_bound) & (df[column] <= upper_bound)]

Other Possible Causes

Beyond leaks:

  • Air Bubbles: Could increase resistance or distort pressure readings.
  • Sensor Errors: Overreading pressure sensors or underreading flow sensor could exaggerate PDiff.

Residuals Skew: Smaller PDiff at Higher Flow Rates

The residuals plot shows a right-shifted skew: at higher flow rates, the actual PDiff is smaller than predicted, indicating overprediction. Leaks between the needle and flow sensor don't explain this.
